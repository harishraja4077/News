Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?

Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription from your reader profile at any time with a single click. There are no cancellation fees, and your premium access will continue until the end of your billing cycle.

Do you offer special rates for students or academics?

Yes! We offer a 50% discount on the premium subscription for students, university faculty, and researchers. Please send a request via our Contact Hub using a valid institutional email (.edu, .ac.uk, etc.).

How secure is the transaction processing?

Extremely secure. All payment data is processed using fully tokenized SSL systems, keeping bank details safely isolated from our storage. We support credit cards, PayPal, and Apple Pay.

Is ad-free reading available on mobile applications?

Yes. Once you upgrade your account to Premium, logging in on any device (phone browser, mobile app, desktop, tablet) will automatically remove all banner ads, trackers, and sponsored scripts.
